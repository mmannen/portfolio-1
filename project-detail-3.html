UniGuiden is a comprehensive website designed to provide all the essential information about university admissions and life after graduation. The homepage features a search function to find specific programmes, with the most frequently searched options displayed as shortcuts. Visitors can explore which master’s programmes are
available based on a particular bachelor’s degree, as well as the types of careers those studies may lead to. The platform also includes detailed admission information, not only for Swedish universities, but for institutions across the Nordic region.
The idea for UniGuiden emerged from my own experience while researching master’s programmes. Although I had a general sense of the field I wanted to pursue, I found it challenging to understand exactly what different programmes offered, what career paths they could lead to, and which ones I was eligible to apply for. This inspired me to
develop a clear and accessible platform that brings together all relevant information about university studies in one convenient place.

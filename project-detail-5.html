This infotainment system was designed for the Nissan Ariya, with a strong focus on ease of use and safety while driving. It allows drivers to conveniently manage music and radio, or switch to their preferred podcast app. Navigation and climate control functions are easily accessible via the navigation bar on the left side of the screen,
minimizing the need for the driver to reach or divert their attention from the road. If needed, drivers can also make hands-free calls, further supporting a safe and seamless driving experience.
As part of the course "Cognitive Design", my team and I developed this infotainment system based on principles from neuroscience and psychology. Every design decision required justification using current research to ensure that the system effectively fulfilled its intended purpose.

Trendy is an application designed to make sustainable shopping easier. It allows users to view how environmentally and ethically responsible different brands are, based on factors such as transportation, working conditions, and recycling practices. The app also tracks users' purchases, which is why logging in with BankID is required, and uses that data to calculate
how sustainable their shopping habits have been each month. Users can also view how their shopping behavior has changed over time.
Trendy was one of the first applications I designed during my time at university. Working in interdisciplinary teams of cognitive science students and civil engineering students, we collaborated to develop applications focused on sustainability. Our slightly different approaches to the problem complemented each other well. After researching various
sustainability challenges, we identified the clothing industry as a major contributor to environmental and ethical issues. This insight led us to focus our project on creating a solution aimed at improving consumer habits within that sector. This project was part of the course Human-Computer Interaction.
